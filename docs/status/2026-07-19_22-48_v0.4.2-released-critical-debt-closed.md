### Phase D — Critical fixes (v0.4.2-bound)

1. **`fuzz_hooks` semver leak closed.** Replaced `#[doc(hidden)] pub mod fuzz_hooks` with `#[cfg(any(test, feature = "fuzz"))] pub mod fuzz_hooks`. `#[doc(hidden)]` hides items from rustdoc but does NOT remove them from the semver surface; the `#[cfg]` gate does both.
